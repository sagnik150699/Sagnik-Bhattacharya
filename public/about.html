About Sagnik Bhattacharya

Building developers who can ship real products.

I’m Sagnik Bhattacharya, founder of Coding Liquids and a tech educator focused on hands-on, project-based learning. I create courses and training programmes that help students and teams move beyond theory and build production-ready skills with real-world tools, workflows, and standards.

Founder · EducatorKolkata, India

Hands-on learning built for real product work.

I started Coding Liquids in 2018 because too much technical learning stops at theory. I wanted to create training that shows how real products are built, using practical workflows, modern tools, and standards that hold up outside the classroom.

Today, my courses on Flutter, Dart, and Excel with AI have reached more than 30,000 students across 175 countries. I also work with teams through consulting and training programmes, helping them strengthen delivery, development standards, and practical problem-solving.

That mix of teaching and consulting keeps the material grounded in real work. The focus is not just on getting something to run, but on understanding architecture, tooling, debugging, and delivery well enough to build with confidence.

Whether someone is learning on their own or upskilling a team, the aim is the same: move beyond theory, build useful products, and develop skills that are ready for real-world work.
